# Privacy Policy

RadioHub is a locally operated application.

## Data Processing

- RadioHub stores all data exclusively on your local device
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers
- Radio streams are loaded directly from the respective stations

## External Services

RadioHub uses the following external services:

- **Radio Browser API** - Public station database (radio-browser.info)
- **iTunes Search API** - Podcast search (Apple)
- **Fyyd API** - Podcast search (fyyd.de)

The respective privacy policies of these services apply when using them.

## Cookies and Tracking

RadioHub does not use cookies or tracking.
